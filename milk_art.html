MILK ART

MATERIALS REQUIRED:

  • A bowl
  • ½ cup milk
  • Dish soap
  • Cotton swab
  • Food coloring (more than one color)
  • Pepper (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Pour the milk into the bowl. Be careful not to move the bowl; you want the milk as still as possible.
  2. Put one drop of each color in different places in the milk.
  3. Put just a tiny amount of soap on the end of the cotton swab, then touch it to one of the colors.
  4. Let the experimenting begin!
  5. To clean up, just pour the milk down the drain (do not drink).

HOW IT WORKS:

Milk has fat in it and food coloring floats on the top of the fat. The fat is all connected with bonds. Think of it as a little piece of it all holding hands with each other. Dish soap acts like an “unhinging” agent because it breaks the bond, allowing them to separate. When you add the dish soap to the milk, the fat separates and moves, making your magical milk art!
