MASSIVE SOAP EXPAND :

MATERIALS REQUIRED :

  • one balloon

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. Blow up the balloon so that it is filled with air.
  2. Hold the balloon up to your ear.
  3. Lightly tap the other side of the balloon with your finger. Can you hear the sound well? Can you hear the vibrations?
  4. Now let the balloon go!!

HOW IT WORKS :

When you blow into the balloon you are pushing air molecules in the balloon. They are forced into a close area so that they are very close. These circumstances allow the air molecules to carry the sound waves better.
