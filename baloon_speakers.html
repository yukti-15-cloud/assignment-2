BALLON SPEAKERS :

MATERIALS REQUIRED :

  • ivory soap
  • Large microwave-safe bowl

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. Place the bar of soap in the bowl, and microwave it on high power for 2 minutes.
  2. Watch it grow!
  3. Wait 2 minutes for the bowl and foam to cool.

HOW IT WORKS :

Ivory soap has lots of little bubbles in it. As the bubble air is headed in the microwave, they get bigger. The soap is a solid, so once it expands, it stays expanded.
